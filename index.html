Hi I'm Tim

Hey there Holberton, I'm excited to be taking the next step towards a career in engineering.

I am originally from Michigan USA, and grew up in Ypsilanti a small town about half an hour's drive from Detroit. More recently I've called Oakland California home. The header image at the top of the page is actually of Lake Merritt the estuary in the heart of Oakland. The lake is the oldest bird sanctuary in America, and is a crown jewel in the town. On Sundays hundreds of families come down to the lake and picnic in the park. There is food, live music, street preformers, tons of classic cars and tricked out scrapper bicycles. It's everyone's backyard and such a great place to see the whole community enjoying the weekend!
